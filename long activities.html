Activities in San Vicente's Long Beach

Scuba diving

Scuba diving is a mode of underwater diving where divers use breathing equipment that is independent of a surface supply. The term "scuba" is an acronym for Self-Contained Underwater Breathing Apparatus.

Sunbathing

Sunbathing is the practice of relaxing or spending time outdoors while exposing the skin to natural sunlight. People often do it for leisure, to enjoy warmth, or to achieve a tanned complexion.

Island hopping

Island hopping refers to the practice of visiting multiple islands in a relatively short period of time, often through boats or ferries.

Snorkeling

Snorkeling is the practice of swimming face-down in a body of water while breathing through a snorkel, which is a shaped tube, usually accompanied by swimming goggles or a diving mask and swim fins.

Kayaking

Kayaking is a water sport where a person uses a small boat called a kayak and a double-bladed paddle to move across water, ranging from calm lakes to fast-moving.

Skydiving

Skydiving is the sport of jumping from an aircraft and descending to the ground using a parachute, often including freefall maneuvers for recreation or competition.

Mangrove Tour

Mangrove tours are guided eco-adventures that allow visitors to explore mangrove forests, learn about their ecological importance, and experience wildlife in coastal ecosystems.

Paddleboarding

Paddleboarding, also known as stand-up paddleboarding (SUP), is a water sport that involves standing on a large, stable board and using a paddle to propel oneself across the water.

Foods in San Vincente's Long Beach

Tamilok

Tamilok are woodworms found in rotting mangroves, often prepared kinilaw-style (raw with vinegar), tasting similar to oysters.


Kinilaw

A fresh raw seafood salad mixed with vinegar, citrus, onions, and chili.


Ginataang Talong

a traditional Filipino dish where tender eggplants and succulent pork belly are simmered in coconut milk until a rich, velvety sauce develops.


Adobong Pusit

A squid dish marinated and cooked in a savory mixture of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices, creating a unique regional flavor.

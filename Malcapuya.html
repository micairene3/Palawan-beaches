Malcapuya Beach

Malcapuya Beach is a tropical paradise located off the southern coast of Coron town in Palawan Island. It is known for its powdery white sand beaches and crystal-clear turquoise waters, making it an ideal spot for swimming, sunbathing, and snorkeling. The beach is calm and pristine, far from the crowds of more commercialized shores, offering a serene atmosphere and a peaceful ambiance that feels worlds away from the hustle and bustle of daily life. Snorkeling enthusiasts will find coral reefs just offshore teeming with tropical fish, while those who enjoy a bit of adventure can hike up a nearby hill to witness panoramic views of the surrounding islets.

San Vicente Long Beach

San Vicente Long Beach is a hidden gem in Palawan, Philippines, known for its 14-kilometer stretch of white sand and crystal-clear waters. The beach is not only the longest in the Philippines but also the second longest in Southeast Asia. It is a popular destination for those seeking a peaceful and secluded beach experience, away from the hustle and bustle of tourist hotspots. The beach is divided into six shorter stretches by rocky cliffs, offering a unique and varied beach experience.

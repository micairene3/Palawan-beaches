Duli Beach

Duli Beach is a 2.5 km stretch of golden sand located in the scenic El Nido region of Palawan, Philippines. It is known for its quiet atmosphere, clear waters, and lush tropical jungle, making it a perfect destination for those seeking tranquility and isolation. The beach is relatively undeveloped, preserving its natural beauty, and is popular among surfers due to its consistent waves. Visitors can enjoy activities like snorkeling, kayaking, and surfing, while also being mindful of the occasional presence of marine turtles.

Activities in Las Cabanas Beach

Dinning

Dining is more than just eating—it’s an experience that engages all the senses. It’s the comforting aroma of freshly prepared dishes, the visual delight of artfully plated meals.

Ziplining

A zipline is an exciting aerial adventure activity where a person glides along a strong, suspended cable from a higher point to a lower point, propelled by gravity.

Swimming

Swimming is the art and skill of moving through water using coordinated body movements, typically involving the arms, legs, and core. It can be enjoyed as a recreational activity, a competitive sport, or a lifesaving skill.

Snorkeling

Snorkeling is the practice of swimming face-down in a body of water while breathing through a snorkel, which is a shaped tube, usually accompanied by swimming goggles or a diving mask and swim fins.

Paddleboarding

Paddleboarding is a water sport where participants stand or kneel on a stable board and use a paddle to move across the water.

Sunset Viewing

A sunset viewing refers to the act of observing the moment when the sun disappears below the horizon, marking the end of the day and the beginning of night.

Diving

Beach diving, often called shore diving, is a form of scuba diving where divers enter the water directly from the shore instead of using a boat or other watercraft.

Hammock Relaxation

Hammock relaxation is a practice that involves lying in a hammock, which provides a unique and comfortable way to unwind.

Famous Foods in Las Cabanas Beach

Wood-fire Pizza

wood-fired pizza refers to a unique dining experience where pizzas are made in a brick oven, utilizing wood fire for cooking.


Kinilaw

A Filipino dish consisting of chopped raw, grilled, or boiled meat—such as fish, pork, goat, beef, or carabao—marinated in vinegar or citrus juices.


Hambrugers

Hamburger, a food consisting of one or more cooked meat patties, often beef, placed inside a sliced bread roll or bun roll.


Piña Colado

The piña colada is a classic Caribbean cocktail known for its creamy, fruity, and tropical flavor.

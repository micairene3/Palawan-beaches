Activities in Duli Beach

Beachcombing

Beachcombing is the activity of searching and exploring the intertidal zone to discover natural and man-made objects.

Beach Volleyball

Beach volleyball is a team sport played on sand courts, typically involving two teams of two players each.

Kayaking

Kayaking is the use of a kayak for moving over water, distinguished by the sitting position of the paddler.

Surfing

A surfing beach is a beach where the conditions are suitable for surfing, which is a surface water sport where surfers ride on the face of moving waves using a surfboard.

Sea Turtle Release

Sea turtle releases are public conservation events where hatchlings or rehabilitated turtles are returned to the ocean, offering a unique opportunity to witness wildlife recovery and support conservation efforts.

Swimming

Swimming is both a recreational activity and a competitive sport in which a person moves through water by coordinated movements of the arms, legs, and body. It can be performed in various environments.

ATV Tour

An ATV tour is a guided adventure where participants ride all-terrain vehicles (ATVs) through various terrains, often led by experienced guides who ensure safety and provide insights about the area.

Beach Dinning

Beach dining is a delightful experience that combines the charm of a beach setting with a meal. It typically involves enjoying food and drinks outdoors by the sea, creating a relaxed atmosphere.

Foods in Duli Beach

Danggit Lamayo

A crispy, tangy, and aromatic dish made from marinated dried fish, often served with garlic rice and eggs.


Piña Colado

A refreshing drink made with ice cream and a sweetened syrup, popular in the Philippines.


Local Seafood

Duli Beach offers a variety of fresh seafood dishes, including grilled fish and other local seafood specialties.


Grilled Fish

A quick and easy recipe that involves stuffing the fish with butter and lemon slices before grilling.

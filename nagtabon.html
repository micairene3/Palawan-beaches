Nagtabon Beach

Nagtabon Beach is a hidden gem in Palawan, Philippines, located about 30 kilometers northwest of Puerto Princesa City. It is known for its pristine waters, fine white sand, and picturesque surroundings, making it a serene and tranquil escape. The beach is less frequented by tourists, which maintains its pristine and untouched charm, inviting those with a penchant for tranquility and natural beauty.

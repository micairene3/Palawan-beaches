Hidden Beach

Hidden Beach in El Nido is a hidden gem known for its stunning limestone cliffs and crystal-clear waters. It is accessible only by boat, requiring a swim through a small opening to reach the beach. The beach is surrounded by lush greenery and is home to the endangered clownfish, making it a popular spot for snorkeling and eco-tourism. Visitors can book guided tours or take a ferry from El Nido town to reach the beach.

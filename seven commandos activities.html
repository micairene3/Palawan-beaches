Activities in Seven Commandos Beach

Swimming

Swimming is the art and skill of moving through water using coordinated body movements, typically involving the arms, legs, and core. It can be enjoyed as a recreational activity, a competitive sport, or a lifesaving skill.

Beach Volleyball

Beach volleyball is a team sport played on sand courts, typically involving two teams of two players each.

Sunbathing

Sunbathing is the practice of relaxing or spending time outdoors while exposing the skin to natural sunlight. People often do it for leisure, to enjoy warmth, or to achieve a tanned complexion.

Island Hopping

Island hopping is a popular travel activity that involves visiting multiple islands, typically by boat, to explore beaches, lagoons, and marine life.

Kayaking

Beach kayaking is a thrilling and exhilarating way to enjoy the ocean's beauty and experience the thrill of paddling.

Dining

Beach dining is a culinary experience situated directly on the shore or at a beachfront restaurant, combining ocean views, sea breezes, and often fresh, local seafood.

Snorkeling

Beach snorkeling is a fun and accessible way to explore the underwater world. It allows you to swim along the surface of the water with a mask.

Beach Photography

Beach photography is a genre of nature, landscape, or portrait photography focused on capturing the coastal environment, including sea, sand, sky, and wildlife.


Famous Foods in Seven Commandos Beach

Buko Juice

Buko juice is the natural liquid inside a young coconut, serving as nourishment for the developing fruit. It is translucent, low in calories and sugar, and rich in electrolytes and minerals.


Banana Cue

Banana cue is a popular Filipino street snack made from deep-fried saba bananas coated in caramelized brown sugar and served on bamboo skewers.


Fresh Fruits

Fresh fruit refers to fruits that are harvested. It includes a variety of fruits that are rich in vitamins, minerals, and dietary fiber, making them essential for a healthy diet.


Beach Snacks

Snack foods are small portions of food typically consumed between main meals. They are convenient, ready-to-eat items that come in various forms, including chips, nuts, and granola bars.

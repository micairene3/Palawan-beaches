Activities in Nagtabon Beach

Surfing

Surfing is a surface water sport where individuals, known as surfers, use a board to ride on the forward section of moving waves, typically towards the shore.

Swimming

Swimming is the art and skill of moving through water using coordinated body movements, typically involving the arms, legs, and core. It can be enjoyed as a recreational activity, a competitive sport, or a lifesaving skill.

Skimboarding

Skimboarding, also known as "skimming", is a boardsport in which a skimboard is used to glide across the water's surface to meet an incoming breaking wave, and ride it back to shore.

Beachcombing

Beachcombing is an activity that consists of an individual "combing" (or searching) the beach and the intertidal zone, looking for things of value, interest or utility.

Snorkeling

Beach snorkeling is a fun and accessible way to explore the underwater world. It allows you to swim along the surface of the water with a mask.

Sunset Viewing

Sunset viewing refers to the act of watching the sunset, which is often associated with various psychological and physiological benefits.

Sunbathing

Sunbathing is the act of sitting or lying in the sun, often to relax or tan, while gaining potential health benefits from sunlight exposure.

Beach Photography

Beach photography is a genre that captures the beauty of coastal landscapes, people, and wildlife, focusing on the unique lighting and composition challenges presented by beach environments.

Famous Foods in Nagtabon Beach

Fried Rice

Fried rice is made by stir-frying pre-cooked rice in a wok or frying pan, often with ingredients like eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat, and seasoned with sauces such as soy sauce or sesame oil.


Vegetable Salad

A vegetable salad is a dish that typically consists of a mixture of raw or cooked vegetables, often combined with fruits, nuts, seeds, and sometimes proteins like cheese or meats.


Mango Shake

A mango shake is a refreshing fruit drink made primarily from ripe mangoes blended with ice, milk, and sometimes sugar.


Grilled Fish

Grilled fish refers to fish that has been cooked over an open flame or on a grill, which imparts a distinct smoky flavor and a crispy texture.
